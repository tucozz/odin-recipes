Brazilian Misto

Description

A quick and easy recipe that most brazilians eat everyday at the morning or afternoon. It is pretty common to use ham and cheese, but other ingredients such as eggs or ketchup are also welcome (be careful! too much egg and your misto will evolve into a Pao com Ovo!)

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put it all together in your favorite order
  2. Heat it up in a Misteira
  3. Enjoy
